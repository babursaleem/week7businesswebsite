About

Everything we do is in your best interest and we do not want to limit your creativity, your business is our business!

  • Our Humble Beginnings

    We operate out of an office/warehouse in Signal Hill. In fact, Allen Printing & Graphic Design has been printing in Long Beach for over 30 years!

  • Why Us?

    Our philosophy is simple. Everything we do here is custom. You won't find any templates on our computers, and we don't take shortcuts. If you bring a project to us, you can be sure that it's being taken care of by a group of people who care about their work.

  • Access to limitless hands on options for your marketing

    We have a wide array of printing techniques and design tools at our disposal, and we love a good challenge! See something you like and want to use it for your job? Chances are we can tell you what's involved, and if we can't, you can bet we'll find out!

  • Our Support for our community and Local Businesses

    As a not only a business owner but also a resident of Long Beach, supporting the local community is extremely important to me. I do not stop at just getting the product you need but I love keeping in touch with my local clients and you will definitely find me eating at Lucilles or checking out a Gabe Doby live show! That is the beauty about working with the local community, your business is my business and here at Allen Printing we will make it everybody's business!
